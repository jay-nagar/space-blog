Jupiter is the largest planet in our solar system.

More than 1,300 Earths could fit inside Jupiter by volume. It is mostly made of hydrogen and helium, which are the same main gases found in the Sun.

One of the most famous parts of Jupiter is the Great Red Spot, which is a huge storm that has been spinning for hundreds of years.
