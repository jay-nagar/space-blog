Astronauts need food that is safe, easy to store, and not too messy.

Crumbs can float around in spacecraft, so some foods are packaged in a way that keeps them contained. Astronauts can eat tortillas, fruit, vegetables, and desserts.

Some space food is rehydrated with water, and meals are often enjoyed together to help keep crews connected.
